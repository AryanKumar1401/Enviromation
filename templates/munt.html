    About

    With the Coronavirus Pandemic, came severe repercussions for today's youth. We were robbed of opportunities to debate, deliberate and make new friends. However thanks to the internet, not all is lost. With this in mind, Youth Online Model United Nations (YOMUN) was created. YOMUN consists of five amazing committees let by an extremely distinguished and experienced executive board. Our executive board consists of past Director-Generals, Harvard MUN Assistant Directors, and Harvard MUN Best Delegate awardees. YOMUN hopes to encourage co-operative and collaborative debate between all delegates and provide a fun and interesting learning experience. We hope that all delegates take advantage of the extremely experienced executive board to grow as a delegate themselves. Best of all, YOMUN is completely free.
